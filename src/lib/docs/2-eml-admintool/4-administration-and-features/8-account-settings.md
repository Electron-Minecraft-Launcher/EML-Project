# Account settings

The **Account settings** page allows every user (administrators and standard users) to manage their personal profile, update their credentials, and review their assigned permissions.

![EML AdminTool Account settings page](/images/docs/account-settings.png)

## Account information

The top section displays your current profile details. To edit them, hover over the section and click the edit button.

**Username** —  Your display name on the EML AdminTool.

**Password** —  Hidden for security reasons.

**Account type** —  Indicates if you are the Administrator or a Standard user.

## Permissions

This section is read-only. It displays the list of features you are allowed to access and manage.

These permissions are assigned by the administrator when your account is approved. If you need access to a specific feature (like _Files Updater_ or _News management_) but do not see it listed here, you must contact the administrator to update your rights.

## Danger zone

**Delete account** - This button allows you to permanently delete your own account.

> [!CAUTION]
> This action is **irreversible**. You will lose access to the EML AdminTool immediately. If you wish to return, you will need to register again and wait for administrator approval.

> [!NOTE]
> The Administrator cannot delete their own account from here. To reset the main account, a full factory reset of the EML AdminTool is required (see [General settings](/docs/eml-admintool/administration-and-features/general-settings)).
